It all began about 20,000 years ago when an alien race started to take notice of the humans on Kurier. They were very intrigued about their behavior and wanted to find out more about them. In pursuit of their curiosity, they went to the planet and took around 1,000 humans and brought them to the planet of Arco.

Once they arrived on the planet the alien race conducted many tests on the humans. The humans in their current state were not what interested the alien race, but their potential and future evolution are what peaked their interests. After they completed their research, the alien race returned home, leaving the humans alone to fend for themselves.
